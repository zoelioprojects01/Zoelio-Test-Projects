Endangered Species of Antarctica

Emperor Penguin

Scientific Name: Aptenodytes forsteri

Habitat: Sea ice for breeding

Current Population: ~595,000 (Near Threatened, declining)

Subregion: Antarctic coastline, Ross Sea

Cause of Endangerment: Sea ice loss, climate change

Adélie Penguin

Scientific Name: Pygoscelis adeliae

Habitat: Coastal ice-free areas

Current Population: ~10 million (declining in some colonies)

Subregion: Antarctic Peninsula, Ross Sea

Cause of Endangerment: Krill decline, warming waters

Southern Rockhopper Penguin

Scientific Name: Eudyptes chrysocome

Habitat: Sub-Antarctic islands

Current Population: ~1.5 million pairs (Vulnerable, declining)

Subregion: South Georgia

Cause of Endangerment: Climate change, overfishing

Macaroni Penguin

Scientific Name: Eudyptes chrysolophus

Habitat: Sub-Antarctic islands

Current Population: ~18 million (Vulnerable, declining)

Subregion: South Georgia, Antarctic Peninsula

Cause of Endangerment: Predation, disease outbreaks

Antarctic Krill

Scientific Name: Euphausia superba

Habitat: Southern Ocean

Current Population: Declining, exact unknown

Subregion: Antarctic Peninsula, Scotia Sea

Cause of Endangerment: Industrial fishing, sea ice loss

Fin Whale

Scientific Name: Balaenoptera physalus

Habitat: Southern Ocean

Current Population: ~100,000 globally (Endangered)

Subregion: Southern Ocean

Cause of Endangerment: Past whaling, ship strikes

Sei Whale

Scientific Name: Balaenoptera borealis

Habitat: Southern Ocean

Current Population: ~80,000 globally (Endangered)

Subregion: Southern Ocean

Cause of Endangerment: Climate change, overfishing

Wandering Albatross

Scientific Name: Diomedea exulans

Habitat: Southern Ocean, sub-Antarctic islands

Current Population: ~26,000 (Vulnerable)

Subregion: South Georgia, Crozet Islands

Cause of Endangerment: Longline fishing, bycatch

Amsterdam Albatross

Scientific Name: Diomedea amsterdamensis

Habitat: Southern Ocean

Current Population: ~100 (Critically Endangered)

Subregion: Sub-Antarctic islands

Cause of Endangerment: Bycatch, habitat disturbance

Crabeater Seal

Scientific Name: Lobodon carcinophaga

Habitat: Sea ice

Current Population: ~2–5 million (declining in some areas)

Subregion: Antarctic Peninsula, Weddell Sea

Cause of Endangerment: Sea ice loss, krill decline

Weddell Seal

Scientific Name: Leptonychotes weddellii

Habitat: Sea ice

Current Population: ~800,000 (declining in some areas)

Subregion: Ross Sea, Antarctic coastline

Cause of Endangerment: Climate change, human disturbance

Southern Giant Petrel

Scientific Name: Macronectes giganteus

Habitat: Southern Ocean, sub-Antarctic islands

Current Population: ~100,000 (declining)

Subregion: South Georgia, Antarctic Peninsula

Cause of Endangerment: Bycatch, pollution

Antarctic Tern

Scientific Name: Sterna vittata

Habitat: Coastal Antarctica

Current Population: ~50,000 (declining in some colonies)

Subregion: Antarctic Peninsula, sub-Antarctic islands

Cause of Endangerment: Habitat disturbance, climate change

Patagonian Toothfish

Scientific Name: Dissostichus eleginoides

Habitat: Southern Ocean

Current Population: Declining, exact unknown

Subregion: Ross Sea, Antarctic Peninsula

Cause of Endangerment: Overfishing, illegal fishing

Antarctic Pearlwort

Scientific Name: Colobanthus quitensis

Habitat: Ice-free terrestrial areas

Current Population: Declining, exact unknown

Subregion: Antarctic Peninsula

Cause of Endangerment: Climate change, invasive species
